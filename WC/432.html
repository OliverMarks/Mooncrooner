Poland 2-0 Saudi Arabia

Robert Lewandowski ended his World Cup woes firing Poland back into qualification contention against a lively Saudi side.

The Barcelona striker emotionally celebrated his 82nd minute goal that confirmed Polish victory and provided redemption for his penalty miss in their opening fixture. The Poles had taken the lead against the run of play when Piotr Zielinski (39â€™) fired into the roof of the net from a ball cut back from the right.

Things could have been very different as Saudi Arabia started the sharper of the two sides, continuing with the energy and tenacity that led them to victory against Argentina last week. Their first half pressure should have resulted in an equaliser before the half but Wojciech Szczesny first denied Salem Al-Dawsari from the spot and then saved Mohammed Al-Burayk's rebound.

The win for Poland means a draw will be enough in their final match against Argentina to qualify. Permutations are slightly more lengthy for Saudi Arabia when they play Mexico but qualification is still in their own hands.
