Japan 0-1 Costa Rica

Keysher Fuller’s 81st minute goal gave Costa Rica a shock victory against Japan in Group E.

Japan were camped in Costa Rica's half for much of the game and it seemed only a matter of time before their pressure would pay off but in a rare break, Japan failed to clear and Fuller curled a shot which keeper Shuichi Gonda could not keep out.

Fuller’s shot was Costa Rica’s first and only shot on target so far across both their games but was enough to give them an historic first win over Japan. The Blue Samuari’s will be rueing missing a golden opportunity to qualify from a challenging group with a game to spare.

The group is now wide open with all teams still able to qualify, Japan face Spain in their final game whereas Costa Rica will look to upset Germany.
