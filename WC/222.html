Senegal 0-2 Netherlands

Two late goals from Cody Gakpo and Davy Klasson secured the Netherlands the win in their opening game of Group A against Senegal at the Al Thumama Stadium.

Cody Gakpo continued his fine form for the Clockwork Orange beating a flailing Edouard Mendy to head home in the 86th minute. That was the Dutch’s first shot on target in the game but they continued to pile on the pressure and Davy Klasson confirmed victory in the last minute of added time with a tidy finish from a rebounded shot from substitute Memphis Depay.

In a lively affair Senegal missed the clinical finishing of their star man Sadio Mane who withdrew from the squad through injury before the tournament began. The Lions of Teranga had the best of the chances for much of the game, testing Andries Noppert’s goal on multiple occasions but unable to find a breakthrough.

The win takes the Netherlands joint top of Group A with Ecuador who they face on Saturday. Senegal will look to get their qualification hopes back on track against hosts Qatar on Friday.
