Argentina 1-2 Saudi Arabia

In the biggest shock of the tournament so far, Saudi Arabia came from behind to beat pre-tournament favourites Argentia with a superb performance in their Group C opener.

In a blistering 10 minute period in the second half Saudi Arabia overturned a 1-0 deficit thanks to a well taken finish from Alshehri (48’) and a wonderful strike from the edge of the box by Aldawarsi (53’). The Falcons were excellent throughout, tactically bold and more than up to the challenge posed by the two time world champions.

Argentina started brightly with a Lionel Messi penalty handing them the lead after 10 minutes. Although they had three goals ruled out through VAR, they failed to build on their lead before the break. After falling behind, Argentina struggled to create clear chances as the Saudi’s defended bravely till the end to hold on for a win that will no doubt find its place amongst the biggest upsets in the tournament’s history.

The Albicelestes now face a crucial game against Mexico on Saturday. Saudi Arabia will look to all but cement qualification when they play Poland in their next group game.
