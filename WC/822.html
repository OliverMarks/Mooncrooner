Brazil 2-0 Serbia

A Richarlison double in the second half gave pre-tournament favourites Brazil an impressive win against Group G rivals Serbia.

It took an hour for the number 1 ranked team to get going but once they did they showed why they are considered by many as title contenders. Richarlisonâ€™s smart finish from a Vinicious Jnr cross broke the deadlock but his second was a sight to behold. Receiving the ball with his back to goal in the centre of the box, the Spurs forward flicked it up and produced a wondrous acro-scissor-cycle strike to ensure the Little Canaries victory.

Serbia will be disappointed by the result but can take positives from a solid defence performance in the first half that frustrated the attacking talents of their opposition. The strength of the Brazilian squad was on full display Jesus, Antony, Rodrygo and Gabriel Martinelli all joined from the bench to participate in the carnival of chances.

Brazil will qualify if they win their next game against the Swiss. The Eagles go again against Cameroon on Monday.
