Belgium 0-2 Morocco

The shocks continued in this World Cup as a brilliant Morocco upset pre-tournament favourites Belgium at the Al Thumama Stadium.

The win, Morocco’s first World Cup victory in 24 years, was thoroughly deserved. Abdelhamid Sabiri’s free kick from near the corner flag caught out Belgium goalkeeper Thibaut Courtois at the near post as it curled in for the 73rd-minute winner and the points were secured when Zakaria Aboukhal scored their second in stoppage time on the counter attack.

Belgium, who had scraped a 1-0 win over Canada in their opening game, again looked a pale shadow of the team who finished third in 2018 and climbed to second in the world rankings.

They must now battle for a last-16 spot against Croatia in their final match. Morocco sit joint top of Group F and play already eliminated Canada with qualification in their control.
