How to write a report in ceefax

The top paragraph of a really good ceefaxx match report should endeavour to tell the key story of the match, such as who scored the winner.

it should also say which sides are playing and why it matters, for example if a win means promotion for a team.

the next three paragraphs should describe in as much detail as possible every goal and the major incidents.

Write four paragraphs totalling about 80 words, with a minimum of two lines and a maximum of four.
