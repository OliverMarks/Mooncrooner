Wales 0-3 England

A fine second half display saw England book their place in the 2022 World Cup round of 16 after beating Wales 3-0.

Marcus Rashford opened the scoring with a stunning free kick just after the break and within moments Phil Foden tapped home a second. Rashord added a third when his driving run and shot were too hot for Danny Ward to handle.

The Welsh simply could not compete against a proper football team and struggled to string a pass together. Star men Aaron Ramsey and Gareth Bale were nowhere to be seen, as their decline was laid bare for all to see. Such was the impotence of Bale he was hooked at the break (MC acknowledges some outlets claim this was for an injury), As their captain made his way to the nearest golf course, his teammates collapsed like a Souffle left out for too long.

It’s been a long wait for Welsh fans to see their side at a World Cup and they may require a longer wait to get over their dismal performances. England fans on the other hand will be frothing at their chances this tournament, clearly one of the strongest squads in Qatar, they’ll be likely to make light work of their R16 opponents Senegal.
