Qatar 1-3 Senegal

Qatar’s hopes of World Cup glory on home soil were dashed by Senegal as they were the first team to be eliminated from the group stage.

Goals either side of the break gave Senegal the advantage in the tie. Boulaye Dia took advantage of a mistake at the back to take the lead for Senegal just prior to half time before their advantage was doubled by a well taken header from Famara Diedhiou 3 minutes after the restart.

Qatar played themselves back into the game forcing Chelsea goalkeeper Edourard Mendy to make a number of saves. The Maroon were finally rewarded when Mohammed Muntari headed into the net to give the home crowd hope. Alas this was duly doused when Bamba Dieng coolly finished to secure the points for Senegal.

Qatar’s elimination was confirmed due to the result in the later game between Netherlands and Ecuador. They will try to save face when they play the Netherlands in their final match. The Lions of Teranga sit third in the group, only one point behind the leaders, and have a crucial match against Ecuador in the race for qualification.
