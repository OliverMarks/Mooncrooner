Spain 7-0 Costa Rica

A scintillating Spanish side took no prisoners to record the biggest win of the tournament so far, thumping Costa Rica 7-0.

The Spanish dominated from the start and camped out in Costa Rica’s final third for the majority of the game. It was a match to forget for veteran Costa Rican keeper Keylor Navas as Ferran Torres netted twice and Marco Asensio, Dani Olmo, Gavi, Carlos Soler and (even) Alvaro Morata were also on the scoresheet.

It’s difficult to understate how off the pace the Los Ticos looked, a far sight from the sort of high intensity, physical performances that have characterised the shocks of the tournament so far. Luis Fernando Suarez looks to have his work cut out to ensure they do not become the whipping boys in a competitive group.

La Roja will be full of confidence as they head into their next game against a German side who have been humbled by the Japanese. Costa Rica face the Blue-Samurais and will be looking to get their World Cup back on track.
