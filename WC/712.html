Morocco 0-0 Croatia

Morocco held 2018 finalists Croatia to a goalless draw in a turgid affair to open Group F at the Al Bayt Stadium.

Morocco will be the more satisfied of the two sides who both struggled to create real chances in a tightly fought contest. Of the few shots on target both teams managed, Nikola Vlasic came closest, denied by the right leg of Yassine Bounou after striking from the back post from a ball swung in from the left.
