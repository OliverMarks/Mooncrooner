Qatar 0-2 Ecuador

An Enner Valencia double doused Qatary celebrations in the opening game of the 2022 World Cup in Qatar.

The former West Ham forward had a header ruled out for offside in the opening exchanges before calmly slotting in from the spot in the 16th minute. Valencia doubled his team’s advantage with an excellently taken header from a Preciado cross just after the half hour mark.

The home side became the first host nation to lose their opening fixture of a World Cup and never looked likely to trouble the Ecuador goal. Qatar looked lost on the big stage, failing to produce a performance anywhere near the sort that led to their 2019 Asian Cup victory.

Felix Sanchez’s side will look to bounce back against African Cup of Nations champions Senegal in their next game. Ecuador face the Netherlands in what could prove a crucial game in the race for qualification.
