Germany 1-2 Japan

Japan stormed back from behind to shock a punch drunk German side who will be ruing missed chances in their opening fixture of Group E.

In what can only be described as a blitz, substitute Ritsu Doan honourably struck the equaliser with 15minutes to go, 8 minutes later Takuma Asano stunned the former champions, acutely smashing in to send the ever-cleaning Japanese faithful wild.
