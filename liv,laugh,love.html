Liv, Laugh, Love

11/11/2011

Phil shuffled about in his seat nervously
He had a feeling he wouldn't be able to show off any of the rhyming slang he'd prepared
"and what about 9/11 Phil, are you gonna apologise for that?"
The journo sneered, he was enjoying this, all the boys were.
"yes, well obviously my heart goes out-"
Before he could finish another chipped in.
"and the treatment of the Yemenese in the 60's?"
"well of course-"
And another; it was lion's at the watering hole at this point
"Will you finally own up and apologise for the extermination Troglodytes?"
Sweat dripped down Mickleson's face, he looked towards Norman stood at the back of the room for a way out
but Greg just shook his head and wiped his arse with a £50 note.
